

Privacy Policy
Introduction
Search Port is a browser extension that provides a customizable homepage with quick search options. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our extension.

Data Collection and Usage
Storage
Search Port uses browser storage to save your custom search engines and the positions of your search boxes. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Permissions
The extension requires the following permissions:

Storage: To save your custom search engines and search box positions.
Data Sharing
We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties. All data is stored locally on your device and is only accessible by the Search Port extension.

Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
